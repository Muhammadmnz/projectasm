About School

This school was established in 1976 as a result of the efforts of the people of this area under the leadership of the then Perinthalmanna MLA and the legendary Shriman KKS. 24 divisions are functioning in classes 8, 9 and 10. The higher secondary section was started in 2010.

“ASMHSS is a higher secondary school established in 1974.The school has always been a school that stands out in both academic and extracurricular activities. It is a school that has achieved high success in the fields of sports and science at the district and sub-district levels for many years. Dedicated teachers and management working for the sincerity of the school are taking steps to bring the school's activities to the highest level. Even though it is a school in a rural area, it is a school that is relied upon by a large number of children. All classrooms are smart classrooms. The ASMHSS Higher Secondary School is also a school where children and teachers can live in a very friendly manner.” — Nisam
Maths Teacher
