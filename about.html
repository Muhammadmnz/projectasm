About The School

Learn more about us

Velliyancheri was an educationally and socially backward rural area. After primary education, students had to depend on high schools kilometers away for high school education.Due to lack of transportation facilities due to inadequate roads and vehicles, many children had to end their education with primary education. Especially the further education of girls. Realizing the need for a high school in the Velliyancheri village area, this school was started by the poor family to keep alive the memory of their father Janab Alusahib.It may be appropriate to mention some great persons who have earnestly endeavored to get this school sanctioned.

This school was established in 1976 as a result of the efforts of the people of this area under the leadership of the then Perinthalmanna MLA and the legendary Shriman KKS. 24 divisions are functioning in classes 8, 9 and 10. The higher secondary section was started in 2010.Currently, two batches are working in humanities, commerce and science subjects. About two thousand children are studying in this school in both sections.
